More about us

Our brand is ecofriendly, we use used jeans and recycle them to make new products. The Jean is a product that is quite polluting because it requires denim, which is obtained with cotton. It is therefore necessary to collect a lot of cotton to produce jeans and it is often children who collect it. Moreover the jean is made with a dye, it is indigo and this one is rather harmful for health. It is therefore important to recycle the jeans in order to limit the damages and pollution.
